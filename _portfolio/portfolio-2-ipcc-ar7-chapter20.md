I serve as Chapter Scientist for Chapter 20 ('Poverty, Livelihoods, Mobility and Fragility') of Working Group II of the IPCC Seventh Assessment Report (AR7), 2026-2029.

In this role I support the Coordinating Lead Authors and Lead Authors in assessing the global scientific literature on how climate change interacts with poverty, livelihoods, human mobility, and fragility. The work involves synthesising evidence, coordinating chapter drafting, managing references and data, and ensuring scientific rigour across the assessment process.

**Role:** Chapter Scientist
**Affiliation:** Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC)
**Themes:** Climate vulnerability, poverty, migration, fragility, scientific assessment
